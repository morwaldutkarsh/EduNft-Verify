Verify Degrees with Blockchain & NFTs

EduNFT Verify provides a secure, decentralized platform to authenticate academic credentials using NFT technology.

Explore Platform

How It Works

1. Login with NEAR Wallet

  • Click "Explore Platform" and you'll be redirected to the app interface.
  • Authenticate using your NEAR Wallet credentials securely.
  • The system detects your role (Admin or User) based on your wallet address.

2. Admin Access (Universities / Organizations)

  • Admins are authorized wallets (whitelisted by smart contract).
  • They can upload academic documents as NFTs on the blockchain.
  • Admins can also view, manage, and validate all issued credentials.

3. User Access (Students / Employers)

  • Users can search and view publicly available degree NFTs.
  • No write or upload permissions are granted to regular users.
  • Verification is instant and secure, with on-chain data validation.

Use Cases

For Universities

  • Issue NFT-backed digital degrees that are cryptographically secure.
  • Eliminate fraudulent claims by anchoring records on the blockchain.
  • Automate alumni management and academic verification requests.

For Employers

  • Instantly verify the authenticity of academic credentials shared by applicants.
  • Access records via NFT ID without relying on third-party verifiers.
  • Reduce time, cost, and errors in hiring or academic background checks.

For Graduates

  • Own your degree as a transferable and verifiable NFT.
  • Share credentials globally without needing paper copies.
  • Ensure lifetime access to your academic achievements on-chain.
